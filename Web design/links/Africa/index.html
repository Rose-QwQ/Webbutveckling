Welcome To Africa!

Lake Victoria is the largest tropical lake in the world and the second-largest freshwater lake by surface area—only behind Lake Superior in North America. It spans three countries: Tanzania, Uganda, and Kenya, and is a vital source of the Nile River, which is the longest river in the world.
