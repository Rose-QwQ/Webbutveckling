Welcome To Europe!

Fun Fact about Europe: Europe is home to the smallest country in the world—Vatican City!

It’s an independent city-state completely surrounded by Rome, Italy, and covers only about 44 hectares (110 acres)—that’s less than a tenth of a square mile. Despite its tiny size, it’s the spiritual and administrative center of the Roman Catholic Church and the residence of the Pope.
